Caramel dog

This dog is now recognized as a “raza mexicana de perro” and an important part of Mexican culture.

  • It is known for its loyalty, intelligence, and strong connection with families.
  • The breed has become an important symbol of Mexican identity and tradition.
  • Fun fact: Some people say these dogs always find the warmest spot in the house to sleep.
